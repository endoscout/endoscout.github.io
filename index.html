Meta-Analysis Observes Increased Mortality

A systematic review and meta analysis of randomized controlled trials (RCTs) evaluating paclitaxel‐coated balloons and stents in the femoral and/or popliteal arteries concluded that there is an increased risk of death after application of these devices in this anatomy. Konstantinos Katsanos, MD, et al published the study online in Journal of the American Heart Association
