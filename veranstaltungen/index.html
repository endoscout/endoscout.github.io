1. GOEAST Conference

10/2019 Radebeul

öffnen

The GO EAST conference will explore in its DRUG-ELUTING-DEBATE how the drug-eluting technologies, more precisely the Drug-Eluting-Stents and Drug-Eluting-Balloons, are influencing the daily practice of physicians treating patients suffering from PAD in Germany.

The GO EAST conference will explore in its DRUG-ELUTING-DEBATE how the drug-eluting technologies, more precisely the Drug-Eluting-Stents and Drug-Eluting-Balloons, are influencing the daily practice of physicians treating patients suffering from PAD in Germany. Attendees will hear from some of the most influential names in health who will discuss and debate the most pressing issues related to DEB and DES, icluding the biggest opportunities and challenges for physicians in daily practice.

The GO EAST conference will be closed with the development of a consensus paper regarding the usage of modern drug-eluting technologies in peripheral interventions for treating patients suffering from PAD by common discussion with the auditorium.
